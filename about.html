About me

My name is Rayane and I am a Brazilian archivist, exploring a new side of the world.

I have a degree in History and I worked in a couple of different roles within the cultural heritage sector in São Paulo.

I loved working with films, museum objects and historical documents. Although I was comfortable in my workplace, I needed to focus on my personal life. So, I decided to move to Belgium. It has been a new phase in my life, moving to a different country pushed me out of my safe place.

With keen interest in Film ​History and​ ​Arts, I am a person​ passionate about traveling, meeting people from different cultures and reading books on diverse subjects.
